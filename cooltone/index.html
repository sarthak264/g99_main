10 QUESTIONS TO ASK IF YOU ARE CONSIDERING COOLTONE®

How many CoolTone® treatments are needed?
Clinical studies show the best results with a total of 4 treatments for the abdomen and buttocks treated for 30 minutes, twice a week for 2 weeks. To maintain initial results, we recommend one treatment every 3-6 months.
How long is a CoolTone® treatment session? How many treatments will I need?
A CoolTone® treatment session lasts 30 minutes. Clinical studies were done as a series of 4 treatments total – twice a week for 2 weeks.
What does CoolTone® feel like? Does CoolTone® hurt?
CoolTone® is not painful – it simply feels like a series of strong muscle contractions. Thinner patients may feel CoolTone® treatments more and a decrease in energy. Many patients tend to instinctively smile or laugh during the first few minutes, then the body adapts to the rhythm of muscle contractions and it’s easily tolerated.
What should I expect immediately after a CoolTone® treatment?
The area of treatment may be mildly sore, like a strength-training workout. This may persist for a day following treatment.

Is CoolTone® safe? Are there any side effects from CoolTone® treatments?
Yes, focused magnetic muscle stimulation (MMS) from CoolTone® is considered to be safe. As mentioned previously, mild soreness similar to a muscle workout is normal and expected as a side effect from CoolTone® treatments.
How should I prepare for a CoolTone® treatment?
No preparation is required prior to CoolTone® treatment. As your muscles in the treatment area will be strongly contracting, prepare as you would prior to a rigorous strength training session at the gym. Empty your bladder and avoid a full meal prior to treatment..
