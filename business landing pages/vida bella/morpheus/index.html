Morpheus 8

We start to lose our healthy supply of collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid as we age. As we lose these natural skin supports, the result is wrinkles, saggy skin, reduction of skin volume, and elasticity.

Morpheus 8 is a fractional skin treatment that stimulates collagen production of the underlying layers of the dermis. By targeting the deeper layers of the skin, tissues of the face and body can be remodeled to reveal a more radiant youthful appearance. This treatment is ideal for full-body skin resurfacing, skin tightening, and fat reduction. This non-surgical procedure uses advanced fractional radiofrequency energy (RF) to rejuvenate and remodel the skin.

Morpheus 8 can be used on any areas that can benefit from resurfacing and subdermal renewal. It can be used to treat the face, nasolabial folds, jowls, fine lines/wrinkles, acne scars, periorbital area (eyes), abdomen, stretch marks, thighs, legs, and buttocks. There is little to no downtime and no painful recovery period with this procedure.

Morheus8 can be used on a wide range of skin problems. It improves the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, tightens sagging skin, smooths out uneven skin texture, tres hyperpigmentation, and acne scarring. Use Morpheus 8 to fade stretch marks. You can use Morpheus 8 anywhere on your body. It is most popular when used on the jawline, neck, and face.
