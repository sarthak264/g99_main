Secret RF Microneedling

Secret RF is a new technique for delivering fractional radio frequency (RF) energy to all layers of the skin with minimal damage to the skin's surface. The multi-layered treatment is tailored to each client and addresses their unique skin concern during each personalized treatment.

Our Secret RF combines micro-needling and radiofrequency to provide amazing results and little to no downtime. Because the Secret RF treatment can be customized to each patient’s specific skin concerns, it is safe and effective for all skin types! If you want to treat melasma/hyperpigmentation, fine lines/wrinkles, large pores, poor texture, acne scarring, or stretch marks, this treatment is for YOU! Our aesthetician would be happy to discuss the treatment, address your concerns, and answer your questions during this consultation.
