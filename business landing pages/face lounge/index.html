If you notice that your face is starting to age, you may want to consider non-surgical treatments, such as dermal fillers. Over time, the skin loses valuable components such as collagen, elastin, and hyaluronic acid. Dermal fillers are used to help correct signs of aging. The dermal fillers we use at My Face Lounge are made up of a hyaluronic acid gel, which moisturizes and adds volume to the skin.

If you have fine lines, creases or wrinkles, and want to look younger, you would be a good candidate for dermal fillers.
