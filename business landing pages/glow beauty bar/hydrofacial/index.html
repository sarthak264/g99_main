HydroFacial Facts & Details

What Problems It Solves

A HydroFacial treatment, also known as a hydrodermabrasion treatment, is a solution that produces a brighter and clearer complexion. It is helpful to achieve and maintain smooth skin texture, minimize the appearance of large pores, brighten lack luster skin, prevent acne, minimize fine lines and wrinkles, and nourish the skin among other benefits. When used in combination with other services like dermaplaning, chemical peels, and microneedling the results increase exponentially.

How it Works

A gentle jet stream of a customized cleansing solution is delivered to the skin’s surface using the HydroFacial handpiece. The cleansing solution is mixed to address a variety of skin needs including hydration, brightening, nourishing, clarifying, and anti-aging agents. The jet action provides for focused exfoliation while infusing benefits of the cleansing solutions. At the same time, suction is applied through a vacuum in the handpiece which extracts dead skin cells, product build-up, hardened sebum (oil), skin impurities, and other surface debris.

How to Prepare for a HydroFacial Treatment

To preserve the overall health of your skin and maximize HydroFacial benefits, there are certain conditions that must be met prior to your appointment:

  • At least five days before your appointment you should stop using any retinols, tretinoids, or other skin-thinning products
  • Avoid laser and chemical peel treatments at least two weeks before your appointment
  • Protect your skin from sun and windburn as the service cannot be performed when these conditions are present
  • Avoid dermal fillers, Botox, and other neuromodulators at least two weeks before your appointment; however, these services can be done immediately after your appointment
  • To increase your results, cleanse regularly before your appointment but avoid exfoliation a few days before your appointment
  • If possible, arrive to your appointment makeup free; we can remove it if you arrive with makeup but we prefer to spend the session focused on your treatment

Post-Procedure Expectations

Your skin will appear radiant and smoother following the treatment. Pores will appear smaller and more refined. The valleys of lines and wrinkles will be shallower creating a softer appearance. Discoloration will be slightly less prominent. These results increase when you maintain with regular visits. Memberships are available.

HydroFacials FAQ's

Are HydroFacials painful?

The feedback we get from clients is that a HydroFacial treatment is soothing and relaxing. It is non-invasive and gentle - many fall asleep while the service is performed.

How long does a HydroFacial treatment take?

The basic service takes about 30 minutes. This time is extended when the treatment is combined with other services. Because of the short appointment time, many clients maximize the visit with other services. See our membership page for various maintenance packages.

How Many Treatments will I need?

One HydroFacial session should provide you with immediate results. The results can be maintained with regular visits every four to six weeks. If your skin appearance objectives are more aggressive the HydroFacial machine settings can be adjusted to increase these results with visits every two to four weeks.

Is there any downtime?

There is no downtime. Though makeup may be applied after the treatment we recommend waiting six to twelve hours so the topicals applied have time to penetrate and activate benefits in your skin.

How long will I see the benefits?

The smoothing and hydration benefits of the HydroFacial alone will last for about a week. When combined with a comprehensive daily care regimen the benefits can last much longer. Otherwise, maintaining your results is best achieved with monthly visits.
