Skin Lesion Removal with ThermoClear: Facts & Details

What Problems It Solves

ThermoClear is highly effective at treating skin tags, moles, fibromas, spider capillaries, telangiectasia, keratosis, sebaceous hyperplasia, cherry angiomas, sun spots, age spots, milia, broken capillaries, flat hyperpigmentation spots, cystic acne, blemishes, blackheads, and cholesterol deposits.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Both high and low frequency energy (heat) is used based on your specific skin irregularity. This energy is current is directly channeled into, on top of, or around the skin imperfection using a fine, sterile, stainless steel probe. The current facilitates electrodessication (the drying of tissue by a high-frequency electric current applied with a needle-shaped electrode).

IS IT PAINFUL?

We apply a medical grade numbing cream prior to your procedure. This greatly minimizes discomfort. Most clients rate their discomfort between 1 and 3 on a scale of 1 to 10. Any sensation quickly dissipates once the probe is lifted.

HOW DO I PREPARE FOR SKIN LESION REMOVAL WITH THERMOCLEAR?

Your skin should be in as healthy a state as possible. Treatments cannot be performed on skin that is sun- or windburned. In addition, treatments can only be performed two weeks after receiving chemical peels, or microdermabrasion. For dermal fillers, Botox, or other neuromodulators you must wait 72 hours after receiving these treatments before you can receive a treatment with ThermoClear. There should be no other irritation or infections at the treatment site.

HOW SOON WILL I SEE RESULTS?

Treatment of cherry angiomas, broken capillaries, and spider capillaries can often disappear immediately. Hyperpigmented areas like age spots/sunspots/liver spots can take seconds. Larger irregularities can have healing times with results visible from four or five days up to four weeks.

WHAT CAN I EXPECT AFTER THE TREATMENT?

Directly following your ThermoClear treatment you may experience irritation, redness, and light scabbing on the treated area. After a period of 24 hours you may use makeup as your skin heals.

HOW MANY SESSIONS WILL I NEED?

Most skin irregularities can be treated in just one session but occasionally two sessions may be required to completely remove a lesion.
