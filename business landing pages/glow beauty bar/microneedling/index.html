Microneedling Facts & Details

What Problems It Solves

Microneedling is an effective non-surgical and non-ablative treatment that stimulates and supports collagen and elastin to give your skin a firm, full, and smooth texture.

How it Works

A highly calibrated device that uses 12 to 36 fine needles is used to comfortably create “microchannels” in the skin. These micro-injuries stimulate collagen and elastin production. The effects are boosted with the infusion of topical stem cells, proteins, vitamins, minerals, and other effective ingredients and products.

Post-Procedure Expectations

You can expect a rosy appearance immediately after the procedure. The treated area may feel tight and slightly sensitive to the touch. These conditions usually resolve a few hours after the treatment. The skin will appear normal 2 to 3 days after the service.

Expected Results

You will results in as little as two weeks but healing and collagen remodeling will continue for 6 to 8 weeks before optimal regeneration and repair results are seen.

Treatment Number and Frequency

The number of treatments varies from person to person depending on the skin’s current condition, age, and other factors. A minimum of three treatments is recommended for optimal results though improvement can be seen after just one treatment. More severe skin conditions such as deep lines and wrinkles, acne scars, stretch marks, and cellulite may require as many as six to twelve sessions. Sessions should be spaced 2 to 4 weeks apart.

Microneedling FAQs

What areas can be treated with microneedling?

Microneedling can be performed on the face, neck, chest, arms, legs, and abdomen. The number of treatments will vary based on your desired results, age, treatment area, and the severity of your current skin texture.

What type of results can I expect?

You can expect an improvement in skin texture and minimized depth of wrinkles, lines, scars, folds from stretch marks, and dimples in cellulite. The procedure also assists in the overall tone of your complexion from sun damage, hyperpigmentation, age spots, rosacea, and melasma.

How soon should I see results?

While you may see some results right away the most dramatic change will manifest 4 to 6 weeks after your session. A minimum of 3 sessions are recommended for optimal results.

Will it hurt?

Most clients can comfortably withstand treatment but for deep-depth procedures we apply a medical grade topical anesthetic to ensure your comfort.

How long is the treatment?

We suggest you allow 60 minutes from start to finish.

How much downtime will I need?

You will experience a flushed appearance and slight swelling immediately after the appearance but symptoms will resolve within 2 to 3 days. Makeup can be worn 24 hours after the appointment.
