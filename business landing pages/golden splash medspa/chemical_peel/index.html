Chemical peels improve your skin's appearance and are used to resurface your skin. skin-resurfacing treatments are chemical solutions applied to your skin to remove the top layer of your skin. The skin that grows back after a chemical peel is smoother and younger looking.

In a chemical peel, a solution of different chemicals is spread over your skin, and the solution triggers your skin to blister and peel away. New skin grows back less wrinkled, blemishes are reduced, and your skin is smoother and less wrinkled than your previous skin.

Light Chemical Peels are used to remove the epidermis or the surface layer of your skin. Light peels remedy your fine wrinkles, uneven skin tone, dryness, and even acne. You can undergo a light peel treatment every two weeks.
