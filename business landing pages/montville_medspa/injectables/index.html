FILLERS

Signs of aging are inevitable but with cosmetic injectables and fillers, you can smooth out wrinkles, reduce fine lines, and improve facial volume for a more youthful complexion. Dermal fillers are injectable materials that can add moisture to the skin and fill out the face to make wrinkles disappear. We offer RHA®, Juvéderm®, and VERSA™ dermal filler treatments.

Juvéderm® Volbella™ XC: Designed to re-volumize the lips with a subtle effect and natural-looking results. Also blurs lip lines for an airbrushed effect.

Juvéderm® Ultra XC: Compared to Volbella™ XC, this treatment has a poutier effect. For people who want to add noticeable contours to luscious lips.

Juvéderm® Vollure™ XC: Longer-lasting results than the Juvederm® XC. A strong formulation for those who want an advanced treatment that delivers fresh-looking skin.

Juvéderm® Ultra Plus XC: Corrects folds around the face — from nasolabial folds around the nose to parentheses lines surrounding the mouth. A smooth appearance is achieved by filling in the gaps, not immobilizing muscle.

Juvéderm® Voluma™ XC: Designed to lift the cheeks without surgery. Reintroduces lost volume and smoothes lines in the process.

Juvederm Volux™ XC: A gel implant or dermal filler that is injected in specific areas of facial tissue to add definition or reduce the appearance of lines and wrinkles

VERSA™

Manufactured by Revanesse®, VERSA™ is an FDA-approved hyaluronic acid (HA) dermal filler designed to smooth out fine lines and wrinkles, enhance facial volume, and produce natural-looking results. One key factor that separates VERSA™ from other HA fillers is its minimal post-injection swelling — 24% less swelling when compared to other fillers! VERSA™ is commonly used on the lips, nasolabial folds, tear troughs, and cheeks.

BOTOX®

BOTOX® is the most popular non-surgical cosmetic treatment in the world with millions opting for smoother faces and fewer fine lines every year. This FDA-approved cosmetic treatment is designed to temporarily improve moderate to severe frown lines, crow’s feet, and forehead lines in adults. Botox is no longer used for purely cosmetic reasons; it can now be used to treat migraines, hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating), and TMJ pain.

DYSPORT

Dysport is a purified formulation that has been shown to deliver temporary improvements in moderate to severe frown lines, even with repeat treatments. So, the effect of repeat treatments may be comparable to your first use. With just 1 injection into each of 5 points between and above the eyebrows, Dysport® blocks the signal from the nerve to the muscles, ultimately resulting in a reduction of muscle activity and temporarily preventing contraction of the muscles that cause frown lines.

KYBELLA

KYBELLA is used to permanently diminish small pockets of fat on the body such as a double chin, bra bulges, inner thigh fat, and belly fat. Kybella is an FDA-approved injectable that improves the appearance of double chins by destroying fat cells. Kybella is a man-made form of deoxycholic acid, a naturally occurring material that helps the body absorb fat. Treatment with Kybella is fast, requires little downtime, and destroys fat cells permanently.

PDO THREADS

PDO Thread Lifts are non-surgical and minimally invasive. There are no incisions, and no scars are visible. Thread lifting is considered a middle ground between surgery and fillers. It is a unique advancement in non-surgical face rejuvenation. PDO Thread Lifts are safe and generally painless, and you can leave the clinic within minutes after the procedure. Thread Lifts may take three to six months for collagen and elastin to rebuild and lift your face.
