CoolSculpting®️ Elite Body Sculpting

Sometimes no amount of dieting or exercising can get rid of fat in certain areas of the body. Using cryolipolysis, or freezing fat cells, CoolSculptingsup®️ Elite can shape and contour those areas of fat that just won’t go away.

How Does CoolSculpting®️ Elite Work?

Weight loss and diet can remove unsightly fat, but many people still have areas of fat cells that refuse to budge. CoolSculpting®️ Elite freezes fat cells without damaging the skin, giving you the body sculpting you need.

Treatment with CoolSculpting®️ Elite kills about 20% to 25% of targeted cells. The body takes around three months to finish clearing out the dead cells, although you may see results sooner.

CoolSculpting®️ Elite can treat many areas of the body where people have trouble losing difficult fat, including the arms, back, thighs, and jawline. Many of us can never diet or exercise these areas of fat away. Treatment with CoolSculpting®️ Elite is permanent, and dead fat cells never come back. The procedure will start working in one session, but treatment providers recommend three treatments to the area for maximum lasting results.

Can I Get CoolSculpting®️ Elite Body Sculpting?

If you need to get rid of fat in parts of the body where diet and exercise aren’t helping, and if you’re looking for a non-invasive treatment that doesn’t harm your skin, CoolSculpting®️ Elite body sculpting may be right for you. CoolSculpting®️ Elite cannot safely be used for weight loss treatment.

Body sculpting with this procedure can fit into almost any schedule and will not keep you from getting back to work or other activities quickly. CoolSculpting®️ Elite has been demonstrated to be very safe and most people see a good response to the recommended course of treatment.

What Will My CoolSculpting®️ Elite Treatment Be Like?

You may feel some slight discomfort or your skin may feel very cold during the CoolSculpting®️ Elite treatment. Most people find it very tolerable.

CoolSculpting®️ Elite has dual applicators to make treatment faster than ever, getting you in and out of your procedure with efficiency. You should expect to have three treatments on an area to get the best results.

Your treatment provider will use pulses from the applicator to send the cold treatment to the fat under the skin. This causes the fat cells to freeze and die so the body can filter them out and remove them. Your skin cells remain unharmed.

What Happens After CoolSculpting®️ Elite?

After your body sculpting treatment, your skin might feel a little flushed or look red for a few hours. This will go away by itself as long as you follow your doctor’s sun protection recommendations.

With CoolSculpting®️ Elite people can get back to their regular activities immediately, with no downtime or recovery time.

What Results Will I Get With CoolSculpting®️ Elite?

CoolSculpting®️ Elite kills fat cells, but it takes the body about three months to finish reabsorbing them. Three treatments will give you the best contouring and body sculpting.

Each treatment kills up to 25% of the fat cells in the targeted area. With repeated treatments, more fat cells die and the body can remove them. With time, you will be able to enjoy your smoother, more contoured results .
