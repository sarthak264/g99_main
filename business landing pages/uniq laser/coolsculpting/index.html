CoolSculpting is based on the proven science of cryolipolysis, which actually cuts down the number of fat cells in a given area. Also known as “fat freezing,” CoolSculpting treatments use controlled cooling to freeze fat cells, which then die off and decrease the total amount of fat in the targeted area of the body. The effectiveness of such treatments has been proven through years of research and numerous clinical studies.

CoolSculpting has proven effective for men and women of all ages.
