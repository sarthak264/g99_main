BROW LAMINATION

Brow Lamination, also known as a Brow Flip or Brow Lift, is a brow treatment that reroutes hairs to create volume and fullness while giving the eyebrows a brushed-up appearance. It can last up to 5-8 weeks if cared for properly and has a proven track record of restoring symmetry to over-tweezed or unruly brows.

With brow lamination, your unruly brow hairs are smoothed out while also being lifted in a more vertical direction. As a result, your eyebrows are super smooth, as if they had been applied with brow gel.

Brow Lamination is a non-invasive, semi-permanent process that temporarily alters the direction of the natural brow pattern for a fuller and fluffier appearance.
