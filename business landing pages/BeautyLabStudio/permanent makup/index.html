Lip blush

The Lip Blush technique enhances the natural lip color and shapes the lips with a semi-permanent makeup application. It can be used to prolong the effect of lip color, cover unsightly lips or hyperpigmentation. Lip Blush is completely safe and there are no side effects or risks associated with it. If properly cared for, you will have beautiful lips that look great in any social setting or event.

Choose from a wide selection of some of the prettiest lip colors, from natural to bold! You will have a consultation before your first appointment so the artist can assess your natural lip color and any existing pigment in your lips. We will go over options for your treatment and what steps need to be taken to get you the results you want.

The pigment is gently inserted into your lips using microneedles that are finer than a hair follicle. The results are natural-looking and safe for all skin types. Lip Blush is completely customizable to fit your preference in shape and desired level of color intensity.

The process usually takes anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the desired effect, and needs about a week of healing time so you can get your best look!

Powder Eyebrows

Powder Eyebrows are a semi-permanent eyebrow tattoo that has a soft powdery, airbrush finish! They help make the eyebrows appear fuller and create a perfect shape to accentuate your eyes beautifully They will last anywhere from 1-2 years and can be touched up as needed.

Powder brows are perfect for those looking for a quick fix to sparse eyebrows, over-tweezing, or alopecia. Results will last about one year+, then fade out gradually, giving your eyebrows that ‘just done’ look!

Having a Powder eyebrow procedure will give the illusion of naturally full eyebrows, creating a perfect shape to accentuate your eyes beautifully! Powder brows are semi-permanent, but they last longer than traditional eyebrow tattoos.

Microblading

Microblading is often referred to as 3D brows. The manual technique of microblading, considered semi-permanent makeup, is an alternative to the machine technique for creating hair strokes on eyebrows.

Manual microblading pens use sterile packaged blades. Hand-held blades glide gently over the skin, depositing pigment color on the skin’s top layer (epidermis), leaving a trace that is almost difficult to distinguish from human hair. According to the style of your eyebrows and your own preferences, The microblading artists at Beauty Lab Studio will work with you to create the perfect eyebrow shape that will enhance your facial features and match your unique style.

Microblading is a fantastic option for anyone that is looking to enhance the look of their eyebrows. The results are natural-looking strokes resembling hair, regardless of hair density. Microblading is a great solution for people suffering from health problems that cause hair loss and those who are simply unhappy with their eyebrows.
