A Softer And Rejuvenated look With Botox

Botox is the perfect solution to treat and prevent fine lines, wrinkles, and deep facial furrows. They work well on crow’s feet, horizontal forehead lines, and frown lines between the eyebrows. Most procedures take only 10 – 15 minutes, allowing you to return to your daily activities in a flash!

When it is injected into a muscle, Botox stops the nerve impulse from reaching the muscle, thus preventing it from contracting as strongly.This causes the muscle to weaken and relax, allowing the overlying skin to soften and causing wrinkles in the area to disappear. Botox has been in use for many years and is approved by the FDA for the treatment of areas such as glabellar wrinkles (frown lines), crow’s feet, and forehead wrinkles.

Many people begin to see these wrinkles in their mid-30s and 40s.

The earlier you start to treat your skin and reduce facial lines, the greater the chance of preventing permanent damage to your skin and premature aging.
