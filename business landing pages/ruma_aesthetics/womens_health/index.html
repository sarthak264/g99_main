Urinary Incontinence is the involuntary leakage of urine. It is a common and often embarrassing problem. Urinary incontinence isn’t just for those who are aging, it can affect pregnant and postpartum women, and those who are obese, as a result of changes in lifestyle, dietary issues, or medical conditions.

Vaginal dryness is caused when the thin layer of moisture that coats the walls of the vagina, changes. This is often the result or hormonal changes that cause the vaginal walls to thin, meaning they have fewer cells to secrete moisture. This decrease in moisture is what leads to vaginal dryness

Vaginal Laxity often occurs during aging, childbirth, and with certain medical conditions that affect the level of collagen production in the vaginal walls. With vaginal laxity comes decreased libido, lack of sexual arousal, and a reduction in sexual satisfaction.

RUMA Aesthetics offers many levels of treatment to address these concerns. With a consultation, your provider can determine the appropriate treatment for your specific needs and develop a treatment plan with you to meet your individual goals. You don’t have to suffer from vaginal issues!

VTONE
VTone is a gentle FDA-cleared technology designed to provide intravaginal electrical muscle stimulation (EMS) and neuromuscular re-education to rehabilitate weak pelvic floor muscles for the treatment of stress, urge, and mixed urinary incontinence in women. Think of it as a way to train your body on how to perform the strongest, most controlled kegels you’ve ever experienced! This comfortable treatment takes 30 minutes and is recommended for a total of 6 treatments

FormaV
As women age, their bodies face a host of new changes, from a loss in elastin and collagen to physical trauma associated with vaginal childbirth, to reduced estrogen. This quick and comfortable treatment addresses feminine concerns.The treatment takes about 30 minutes and is totally comfortable. There is no downtime or recovery after this treatment!

Morpheus8V
This fractional radiofrequency microneedling technology is designed to provide intravaginal sub-epithelial remodeling. The treatment addresses the following feminine concerns: laxity, painful intercourse, decreased libido, decreased sensation or dryness.

AVIVA
Aviva is a great treatment for women who are self-conscious or unhappy with the way their genital area appears. The two main reasons for having this procedure are discomfort and esthetics. our Aviva procedure dramatically reduces the recovery time, uses a local anesthetic and requires no stitches. It’s easy, safe, and gives women back the peace of mind about their genital appearance.
