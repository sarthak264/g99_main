PDO Threads

PDO Thread Lifts are non-surgical and minimally invasive. There are no incisions, and no scars are visible. Thread lifting is considered a middle ground between surgery and fillers. It is a unique advancement in non-surgical face rejuvenation.

PDO Threads will return the V-shape of your face. It creates a scaffolding that supports the tissues that have inverted or diminished because of aging. It delicately lifts tissues and repositions your skin to recreate a firmer face.

A PDO Threads is a treatment that lifts and tightens sagging skin tissues using threads made of polydioxanone. The lifting thread is introduced deep into the layer of the skin and produces three effects:

  • Instant skin lifting through mechanical effects.
  • Cellular renewal through collagen stimulation and neovascularization to improve texture, fine lines, and wrinkles.
  • Skin tightening by approximating tissues.

PDO threads have been successfully used in surgery since the 1980s. A medical-grade thread is inserted into the target area in thread lifts. The thread lifts the skin, triggers collagen renewal, and helps the treatment area become plumper and younger-looking. Around six months, your body absorbs the medical-grade thread, and the body washes the threads away naturally through hydrolysis.

Before & After

A face contoured by PDO Thread Lifting retains its young and tight skin for up to two years due to the cellular rejuvenation effects. PDO thread treatments are safe, and effective and will leave you with firmer, less wrinkled skin.

Thread Lift can be used anywhere on the body, but it works best on the face and neck. PDO threads can also be used to lift and tighten skin on the upper arms, stomach, above the knees, and thighs.
