Our Top Services

Botox and Dysport

Botox and Dysport are both used to treat and prevent wrinkles, fine lines, and deep facial furrows. By working beneath the surface, Botox and Dysport help reduce the underlying muscle activity that causes the skin to appear wrinkled or folded.

Fillers

The facial fillers used at RUMA are made from hyaluronic acid. The use of fillers can eliminate wrinkles, plump and hydrate lips, replace soft-tissue volume loss, raise scar depressions, and volumize creases and folds of the face.

Facial Treatments

A true RUMA experience is where medically driven results meet luxury spa relaxation. Each of our facial experiences includes products from our medical and organic product lines, a mechanical form of exfoliation, massage, LED, Hydrojelly masks, hydrating lip treatment, and Jane Iredale's custom foundation application.

PDO Threads

PDO Threads will return the V-shape of your face. It creates a scaffolding that supports the tissues that have inverted or diminished because of aging. It delicately lifts tissues and repositions your skin to recreate a firmer face.

Woman's Health

Female intimate wellness is one of the fastest-growing segments of anti-aging. Rejuvenation and restoration of the function and well-being of women’s health can be addressed at RUMA Aesthetics. Our women’s health treatments use the latest technology and methods with radio-frequency and PRP. We believe that women’s health anti-aging is an important aspect of improving women’s everyday lives.

With a consultation, your provider can determine the appropriate treatment for your specific needs and develop a treatment plan with you to meet your individual goals.
