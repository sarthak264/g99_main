What are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers tend to be one of the most requested anti-aging treatments today, due to their ease of use and their ability to create a variety of quick improvements. Dermal fillers help to smooth away facial lines and replenish lost volume and fullness in the face. They can also correct nagging imperfections that aren’t necessarily associated with aging, such as shallow scarring and facial asymmetries. Dermal fillers can be integral for those with early signs of aging when used alone, or as a component in facial rejuvenation surgery to enhance results further.

Dermal fillers are a good option for men and women looking to turn back the clock or camouflage imperfections without incisions or downtime. These formulas work on all skin tones and types, and an array of products allow for them to be used safely and successfully on nearly any area of the face.

Benefits of Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers offer a host of benefits to the right patients.

  • Add volume to the lips
  • Fill in shallow contours
  • Soften facial creases and wrinkles
  • Elevate recessed scars
  • Correct minor asymmetries
  • Reverse hollowing in the midface
  • Minimize early jowl formation on the jawline
