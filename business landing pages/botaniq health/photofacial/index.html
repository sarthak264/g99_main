If you love keeping up with the latest beauty trends, you've probably heard of Radio Frequency technology and its amazing benefits-with EMFace we combine RF technology and HIFES energy. Combined, you have the best of both words and all without surgery or needles.

RF: Monopolar RadioFrequency - Causes homogeneous heating to remodel skin and connective tissue, and increase collagen and elastin without reducing facial fat.

HIFES: High Intensity Focused Electrical Stimulation - Targets 3 'elevator' muscles of the mid-face (Zygomaticus major and minor, and the risorius) and the Frontalis muscle of the forehead with supramaximal contractions which: increases muscle density and quality, resting muscle tone, and reverses muscular aging, by activating the facial nerve and some of its branches.

The combined effect of skin remodeling and muscle toning results in enhanced wrinkle reduction and overall facial lifting.
