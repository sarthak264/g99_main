Botox and Dysport

Soften lines and wrinkles with a short 10-15min procedure. Perfect for forehead lines, the lines between the eyebrows, also known as the "11's" and for crow's feet. This product can also be used for a lip flip, jawline slimming, neck bands and hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating). Botox and Dysport work similarly by temporarily relaxing the underlying muscle, which eliminates the skin being pulled together, causing those lines we all hate. Both products take about 7-14 days to reach the full effect and last about 3-4 months (sometimes longer, sometimes shorter) depending on dose and placement.

Dermal Fillers

Replace lost volume or support with injectable hyaluronic acid. As we age, the bony structures in our faces, flatten and our fat pads fall due to gravity and loss of support. With the use of dermal fillers we can lift that fallen tissue and replace structure and volume. Dermal fillers can also be used to raise or soften scars and lines. Fillers are also a great way to turn back the signs of time that can be evident on the neck and hands. All fillers used here at CurateMD Skin Bar are a Hyaluronic acid, which provides increased safety to our patients.

Skin Pen Microneedling with or without PRP (Platelet rich plasma)

Microneedling causes hundreds of small injuries to the skin, causing an increase of collagen production that can leave our skin looking healthy and young. This procedure is done in the office with topical analgesia, and once the area is numb, only takes about 30min. Great for scars from acne or other trauma as well as fine lines and textural concerns from aging, sun damage or other environmental changes. For added benefits and maximum results we apply PRP to the treatment, also known as a "vampire facial". Skin will be slightly pink and discomfort similar to a sunburn for about 2 days. Skin Pen is the first FDA approved device of its kind.
