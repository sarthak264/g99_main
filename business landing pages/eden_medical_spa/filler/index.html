FILLER

Filler injections are a non-invasive cosmetic procedure. The purpose of fillers is to plump up wrinkles and smooth lines and restore volume to your face.

The use of hyaluronic acid (HA) fillers – such as Versa – fills in sunken areas and reduces the appearance of lines and wrinkles by plumping up the dermis in the targeted areas. These filler treatments have immediate results and last for many months.

FILLER

The injectable product Sculptra is also used to create a youthful appearance, sculpt most body areas, and/or correct imperfections. Sculptra stimulates collagen production by reducing microparticles of poly-L-lactic acid (PLLA) injected into the skin. When compared to HA fillers, Sculptra does not produce immediate results because it takes a while for the patient’s own collagen production to take place in the targeted areas once stimulated by the product.

Before & After

HA fillers provide instant results, so you can see a visible result immediately after your injection. With Sculptra, results will be more gradual, as this type of treatment works in a subtle manner so as not to appear unnatural. Because this type of treatment works over time, it targets the underlying causes of aging, which is too little collagen production.
