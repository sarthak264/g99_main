BBL Photofacial

Light blends in natural colors and makes skin smoother, younger-looking, and more vibrant.

We’re focused.

BBL therapy, photo facial, is a way to improve the color and texture of your skin without surgery. It can undo some of the visible damage caused by sun exposure -- called photoaging.

BBL uses light energy to target a certain color in your skin. when the skin is heated, your body gets rid of the unwanted cells, and that gets rid of the thing you’re being treated for. Unlike lasers, an BBL device sends out more than one wavelength of pulsating light. It can treat a range of skin conditions at the same time.

The BBL Photofacial treatment uses short blasts of high-intensity light to produce younger-looking skin that’s firmer and more even in tone and texture. With little recovery time and no surgery,
