Our Services

Botox/Dysport

BOTOX® Cosmetic and Dysport are used for the temporary improvement in moderate to severe glabellar lines and lateral canthal lines in adult patients. Both Botox and Dysport are neurotoxins made from botulinum toxin type A and manufactured in small quantities to be safe for cosmetic use.

Restylane

Restylane contains a hyaluronic acid in a clear gel formulation. Restylane is made to act like your body's own naturally produced hyaluronic acid that is designed to absorb water, deliver nutrients, and be a cushioning agent. The product helps reduce moderate to severe facial wrinkles and folds and fills the tear troughs, under-eye area, the temples and sunken cheeks.

Juvéderm

Juvéderm is a hyaluronic acid gel containing some local anesthetic to make injections more comfortable. Hyaluronic acid is naturally produced in the body and is considered a sugar. The function of Juvéderm is to nourish and hydrate the skin and act as a cushioning agent. Adding volume to areas of your face is a reliable way to achieve a younger natural look.

Microneedling/PRP

Microneedling, or collagen induction therapy, uses a special device to prick the skin with tiny, sterile needles that stimulate the skin’s natural healing process and trigger collagen production. Collagen helps your skin maintain its structure by holding the skin cells together. As we age, this natural substance begins to break down, which can cause the skin to lose its elasticity and create depressions in the skin.
