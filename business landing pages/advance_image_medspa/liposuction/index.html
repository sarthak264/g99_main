High Definition Liposuction harnesses an advanced technology and technique to achieve the shape you have always desired. Liposuction

Liposuction is the process of sculpting the body by removing fat from specific areas. Almost any area of the body can be treated, and multiple areas may be able to be treated in the same session. High Definition Liposuction is performed using local anesthesia and oral medications eliminating the risks involved with the use of general anesthesia. High Definition Liposuction is tumescent liposuction. Tumescent liposuction is accomplished by injecting a solution into the fat that includes lidocaine for numbing and epinephrine to minimize blood loss. The combination of tumescent fluid and oral medication make it possible to do the procedure without IV/general anesthesia eliminating the risks that come with each. This liposuction procedure is done in the office and you are able to return to daily activities the next day and full activity usually after two weeks. Most people are able to return to work the next day if necessary.
