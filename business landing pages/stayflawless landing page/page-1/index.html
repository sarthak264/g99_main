WHAT IS COOLSCULPTING®?

*Individual Results May Vary

CoolSculpting® is a safe, non-invasive, non-surgical procedure that gets rid of fat by freezing it. With over 10 years of clinical data, this process involves cooling a specific area to the point where the fat cells crystallize. The body will naturally dispose of these fat cells over the course of 8-12 weeks without affecting the tissue outside the area. This is a life-long solution to remove stubborn fat without undergoing the downtime and risks of surgery.
