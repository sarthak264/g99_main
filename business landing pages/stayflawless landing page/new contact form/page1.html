What is coolsculpting ®?

CoolSculpting ® is a safe, non-invasive, non-surgical procedure that gets rid of fat by freezing it . With over 10 years of clinical data, this process involves cooling a specific area to the point where the fat cells crystallize. The body will naturally dispose of these fat cells over the course of 8-12 weeks without affecting the tissue outside the area. This is a life-long solution to remove stubborn fat without undergoing the downtime and risks of surgery. Am i a good candidate for coolsculpting ®?

You’re a good candidate for CoolSculpting® if you’re within 15 to 30 pounds of your ideal weight , and you already follow a healthy diet and exercise habits. CoolSculpting ® works best on the following body areas:

  • Abdomen
  • Back
  • Thighs
  • Upper arms
  • Chest (for men)
  • Underneath the chin
