A dermal filler is a gel-like compound that is injected beneath the skin. A dermal filler creates a smoother or fuller appearance. Dermal fillers are injectable products that fight the visible signs of aging like fine lines and wrinkles.

Fillers contain hyaluronic acid as their active ingredient. The role of hyaluronic acid is to attract moisture into your skin, leading to a moisturizing and plumping effect. Although they work in similar ways, dermal fillers like Restylane, Juvederm, and Versa have some important distinctions.

One of the most gratifying aspects of being treated with dermal fillers is that they offer immediate results. And your results will continue to build over the following days after you receive your injections.

At some point, which varies by product, the injected filler will be metabolized by your body. At that point, you would require follow-up injections to maintain your results.
