The Science Behind CoolSculpting®

Discovered by renowned Harvard scientists Deiter Manstein, MD, and R. Rox Anderson, MD, the CoolSculpting® procedure utilizes cryolipolysis, or freezing temperatures to break down fat cells. The fat cells are then naturally eliminated from the body over the next 1-3 months, resulting in about a 20-25% reduction of stubborn fat in the treated area. It's no wonder CoolSculpting® is the world's #1 non-invasive fat reduction treatment, with over 13 Million treatments performed worldwide.
