Enter coolsculpting Elite

CoolSculpting® Elite has the life-changing technology to get back the body you deserve without needles, downtime or surgery. Developed by renowned Harvard University scientists Dieter Manstein, MD and R. Rox Anderson, MD, CoolSculpting® Elite is a revolutionary FDA-cleared contouring treatment that freezes stubborn fat, which is then naturally eliminated from your body. Cryolipolysis, the science behind CoolSculpting® Elite, was founded on the observation that cold can selectively affect fat cells and gradually destroy them. After this no-needle, no-downtime treatment, results are undeniable and the impact is immeasurable. With CoolSculpting® Elite you can put the “little” back in your black dress!
