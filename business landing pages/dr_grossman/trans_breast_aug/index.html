Transumbilical Breast Augmentation, commonly referred to, as T.U.B.A. or scar-less belly button breast augmentation, is a procedure used to increase the breast size by introduction of breast implants through the umbilicus (navel or the belly button) and then inflating them to a requested size.In the hands of a NYC Plastic Surgeon, Dr. Leonard Grossman, this surgery has proven to be only beneficial, with fewer complications over the traditional methods of breast augmentation by being safer, faster (average time of 20 minutes) and less invasive procedure creating larger breasts without visible scars on the breasts or in the armpit.

Transumbilical Breast Augmentation has many benefits and is the most effective method at delivering the desired results to the patient, with less downtime and post-operative care as compared with traditional surgery. This endoscopic assisted technique has many advantages, among them when the patient wants the breast size substantially increased, not just 1 or 2 cup sizes, but 3 or more. Reason being, there is no tension on the incision line in TUBA.

Frequently patients needing a mastopexy (breast lift) and augmentation can have TUBA, which will lift and enlarge the breasts totally without scars all in one procedure. If you desire larger breasts, need breast reconstruction, do not want scars, or need a lift and are in good physical and mental condition –T.U.B.A. is definitely the procedure for you.

ADVANTAGES OF TUBA:

  • No scars on or near the breast.
  • Breasts look more natural quicker than with any other approach.
  • Chances of losing sensation to the nipple or the breast are considerably smaller than with traditional approach (less than 1% with TUBA).
  • No bleeding complications have occurred in more than 6,000. In traditional breast augmentation there is always some bleeding caused by cutting.
  • Any size implant can be placed the first time around with NO SIZE LIMIT.
  • Quicker surgical time 20-30 minutes.
  • Fewer anesthesia requirements and no post-operative nausea or vomiting.
  • Quicker recovery (many patients return to work the next day).
  • Less pain postoperatively (most patients take nothing “strong” for pain the day after surgery).
  • Implants can be placed under or over the muscle (99% of our patients get under the muscle implants).
  • Fewer capsular contractures occur with TUBA (in our experience less than 1%).
  • Implants can be quickly exchanged the very same way at any time (through the belly button): for new implants, larger or smaller ones.
  • One exception to the above rule: patients, who have capsular contractures, will require open revision surgery.
  • All implants come with a lifetime manufacturer’s warranty.
  • Most patients can resume gym exercise in 5 to 7 days.
