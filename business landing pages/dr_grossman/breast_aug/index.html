Dr. Grossman considers breast augmentation with fat the best procedure as long as the patient has enough fat for harvest and transfer. In the procedure of fat grafting, the patient’s own fat and stem cells are being placed in to the breasts for augmentation with permanent results when done by Dr. Grossman. Fat Transfer or Fat Grafting to the breasts is a safe and reliable procedure when done by NYC Breast Surgeon, Dr. Leonard Grossman. Just like Liposuction of the breasts can result in smaller breasts, adding your own (autologous) fat results in larger breasts.

The procedure of fat transfer in breast augmentation consists of two parts.

  • Using a syringe or specialized vacuum pump and a very small cannula (needle-like instrument), fat is harvested or removed from the areas where it is not wanted: in the thighs, waistline or the abdomen.
  • These fat cells, which have a high content of adult stem cells, are then washed, centrifuged and chemically stabilized for transfer. Live fat and stem cells are then injected in to the breasts scarlessly with a specially modified needle.

Once in the breasts, these fat and stem cells will survive permanently and live just like they did in the area where they were harvested.
