Semaglutide for Weight Loss

Semaglutide is an FDA-approved injection for chronic weight management in adults with obesity or who are overweight with at least one weight-related medical problem. This is the first approved drug for chronic weight management in adults with general obesity or overweight since 2014. It works by acting as the hormone GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) in the body, decreasing your appetite and increasing feelings of fullness.

Semaglutide slows the emptying of the stomach by binding to receptors in the stomach lining. Slow emptying of the stomach signals the brain that it is full, resulting in a decrease in appetite. With weekly injections of semaglutide administered at SeaMist MedSpa, it is possible to lose weight safely and gradually.

  • Lowers blood sugar without causing hypoglycemia
  • Enhances the brain's sense of fullness
