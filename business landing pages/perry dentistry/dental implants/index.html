Tooth loss can be caused by many different things, like trauma or advanced periodontal disease. Tooth loss is prevalent among adults, with more than 178 million people having it. When a person is missing a tooth, it affects their appearance, ability to chew, and confidence. Tooth loss can lead to other oral problems, like tooth decay and bone loss, if treatment is put off. But, believe it or not, it is something you can fix. Dentures have been the most popular way to replace missing teeth for many years. However, patients now realize that dental implants are better for their function and appearance in the long run.

Dental implants are not teeth by themselves! A dental implant replaces missing teeth with a prosthesis. It consists of a small titanium post or fixture inserted into the jawbone, then topped with a crown (cap), a bridge, a partial denture, or full denture. After the implant integrates with your bone, the artificial tooth/teeth are attached to the implant through an abutment.

Dental implants are often thought to be the best way to replace teeth that have been taken out. It’s becoming increasingly popular because they can replace missing teeth in a way that looks and feels more natural.

Humans are “blessed” with two different sets of teeth (our baby teeth and adult/permanent teeth). As the third set of fixed teeth, dental implants can replace a single or multiple missing permanent teeth due to dental decay or gum disease. Implants can last a lifetime and improve your appearance, self-confidence, and your ability to eat what you like and live an active lifestyle. Unlike natural teeth, dental implants are made of titanium, which cannot decay.Implants could be a great way to get back your beautiful, confident smile if you miss one or more teeth.
