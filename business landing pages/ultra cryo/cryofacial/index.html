Cellulite freezing treatment focuses on one area. Cellulite is the herniation of subcutaneous fat within fibrous connective tissue that turns into skin dimpling and nodularity on the pelvic region, limbs, and abdomen.

Cellulite Freezing is a 30-minute treatment (PER AREA) forcing fat cells into apoptosis (cell death), causing the fat cells to disintegrate, resulting in tightening and shrinking of body areas. Cellulite Freezing is most applied to legs and hips. In a brief period, we can help reduce fat in the body.

When fat cells are exposed to cold, they become terminally injured and gradually die. Over time, the body metabolizes and excretes them with absolutely no increase in either cholesterol levels or fat levels in the blood.
