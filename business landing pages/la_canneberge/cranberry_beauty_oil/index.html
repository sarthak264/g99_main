Founder Story

When Dr. Rachel Fidino opened New U Women’s Clinic and Aesthetics in 2020 she reflected on her own personal challenge of trying to find quality skin care products. She was driven on finding the finest products available to sell at the clinic. As hard as she tried, she couldn’t find the right product that was natural and could help with acne, hyperpigmentation, and skin elasticity.

During her research, she discovered that cranberries carry a lot of enzymes which exfoliate dead skin cells while adding a subtle glow to skin. Dr. Fidino spent months researching cranberries and the oil produced from their seeds. In August, she launched, La Canneberge, which is French for “the cranberry.” Cranberry oil is suited for all skin types and has the perfect balance of omega fatty acids 3,6,9, antioxidants, polyphenols, and nourishing vitamins that promote a more youthful appearance. When applied, the oil is weightless, hydrating, anti-Inflammatory, soothing, age-defying, reparative, and clarifying.

The cranberries are organically certified and grown in Washington State. The oil is made from the cranberry seeds and tested prior to being pressed and filtered. Quick absorbing and light on the skin, cranberry oil helps calm irritation and seals in moisture without clogging pores. It’s great for not only the skin but works well on thinning hair, mending split ends and adding hair shine.
