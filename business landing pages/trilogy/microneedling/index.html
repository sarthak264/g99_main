Microneedling or collagen induction therapy involves repeatedly puncturing the skin with extremely fine sterile needles. It sounds invasive, but it really is a minimally invasive procedure designed to smooth away fine lines, and wrinkles, create a more even skin tone, and reduce the appearance of scars and blemishes.

Microneedling is a perfect treatment to rejuvenate the skin.

Microneedling stimulates your body to produce new collagen and encourages cell turnover. It is a successful treatment for skin conditions like melasma, skin pigmentation issues, rosacea, acne issues, hair loss, large pores, sun damage, and loose skin

Collagen is an essential protein that keeps the skin looking youthful, firm, and smooth. Collagen is also lost because of injuries, stretch marks, acne scarring, or other scarring. certain depths of the facial structures and take care of localized facial concerns such as skin laxity, hyperpigmentation, scars, and wrinkles.

Microneedling is not a quick fix. It involves the growth of new skin, which can take several months before the full results of the procedure are seen.

The entire process takes about 30 minutes, and once your provider uses a topical anesthetic, there is little pain. You may need four to six treatments to see a difference in your skin. Talk to your aesthetician about how many treatments you need.
