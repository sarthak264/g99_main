Sylfirm X uses Regional Regeneration radio Repeated Pulse (RP) microneedling to target and treat the abnormal vessels that can lead to melasma, rosacea, and other skin discolorations. With the help of many micro electrodes, and electromagnetic field of energy is created that can penetrate the dermal layer without damaging the tissue.

A robotic system ensures the precise application of the evenly-layered electrodes to reach the desired depth during treatment. By covering all dermal layers, microneedling Treatments are faster, more effective, and more comfortable for patients. Additionally, practitioners can choose from eight modes depending on the goals of the treatment.

Because the Sylfirm X System has such versatility, it can treat a wide range of skin issues. This ranges from basic skin tightening in the dermis to the treatment of pigmentation on the outer dermis. That makes Sylfirm X a great platform for our patients. Sylfirmx is an FDA-cleared RF Microneedling system that can be used to treat these skin issues:

  • Skin Laxity
  • Age Spots
  • Acne Scaring
  • Acne
  • Fine lines and Wrinkles
  • Rosacea
  • Melasma
  • Hyperpigmentation
  • Uneven Skin Tone.

Sylfirm X is so versatile it can be used for most patients, but there are some criteria that would keep a patient from being a good candidate. The following conditions would preclude you from being eligible for Sylfirm X at this time. If you..

  • Are on Accutane for acne
  • Have an active cold sore breakout
  • Have a skin infection
  • Have active psoriasis or eczema
  • Are receiving chemotherapy
  • Are receiving radiation therapy Are pregnant
  • Are taking anticoagulants
  • Have a history of Keloid or hypertrophic scarring

Most practitioners find that three to six treatments, spaced four to six weeks apart provide the optimal experience and outcome for patients. Skin rejuvenation through RF microneedling provides long term increases in collagen in the dermis but does not stop the aging process. That makes it a good idea to continue sylfirm X treatments as an ongoing maintenance treatment twice a year.

For issue such as age spots or rosacea, a single treatment may be all you feel you need. Again, repeat treatments can expand your results.

The actual treatment takes about 30-60 minutes depending on the area(s) being treated.

Some visible tightening results show immediately, as the collagen remodels in response to the microneedles and RF Energy. Larger improvements come as the skin initiates production of new collagen. Most patients feel they really begin to see improvement in the skin at about four weeks, with improvements continuing to build for six months to a year. To continue to help minimize the aging process it is suggested that you continue Sylfirm X treatments as an ongoing maintenance treatment twice a year.

We apply a topical numbing cream prior to your Sylfirm X session. The feeling is described by most patients as comfortable with a warming sensation as the RF energy enters the dermis. Based on the depth and area treated, this feeling can vary.
