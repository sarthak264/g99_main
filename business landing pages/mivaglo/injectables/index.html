BOTOX & DYSPORT

Botox & Dysport is a popular botulinum toxin-based injectable treatment designed to temporarily reduce the appearance of unwanted forehead lines, crow’s feet, and bothersome wrinkles found throughout the face. The procedure is simple and painless, and no downtime is required for patients to achieve dramatic, long-lasting results.

Botox & Dysport uses a powerful neurotoxin to relax tight muscles and reduce fine lines and wrinkles. These FDA-approved treatments prevent the formation of new lines caused by repeated expressions.

FILLERS

Hyaluronic acid, or “HA,” is a natural substance found throughout our body, broken down naturally over time. As we age, we lose these components, and our structural components deteriorate, resulting in premature aging, sagging, and loss of suppleness.

Dermal fillers are a non-invasive treatment that can add hyaluronic acid back to the skin and reverse the common signs of aging. At Mivaglo, we use the popular hyaluronic acid-based fillers JUVÉDERM® and RESTYLANE® to help you achieve your aesthetic goals. The purpose of dermal fillers is to add fullness and shape to hollowed areas, smooth wrinkles and creases, and plump and hydrate the lips. When placed correctly, fillers should create a youthful contour and be undetectable.

KYBELLA ®

No matter how healthy your diet is and how consistently you exercise, losing fullness under the chin can be challenging! The FDA has approved Kybella® as the only prescription injectable approved for eliminating fullness under the chin, often called the “double chin.” The active ingredient, deoxycholic acid, destroys fat storage membranes and prevents fat cells from storing fat. Your body’s natural processes ultimately eliminate or reduce the fat in the treated area, producing a chin profile you’ll love!

KYBELLA® is a non-invasive treatment option that eliminates fat cells only in the treated areas. Our team at Mivaglo will work with you to determine your individual concerns and goals before beginning any treatment.

Benefits of KYBELLA

  • Quick and easy treatments
  • Minimally-invasive
  • Little to no downtime
  • Results are designed to be permanent!
  • Virtually pain-free
