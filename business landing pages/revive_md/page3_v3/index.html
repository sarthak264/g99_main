The Science Behind CoolSculpting

The CoolSculpting procedure utilizes cryolipolysis, or freezing temperatures to break down fat cells. Cold does not damage other cells in the way it does fat cells, and so there should be no damage to the skin or underlying tissue.ells. It’s the #1 treatment in the world for non-invasive permanent fat reduction. Watch the video to learn more and book an appointment for a free consultation to see if CoolSculpting is right for you.

Fat Reduction vs.Weight Loss

After dieting and weight loss, fat cells may get smaller but the number of fat cells remains the same. When weight is gained, fat cells may get bigger again. The CoolSculpting fat-freezing procedure helps to reduce the number of fat cells in the areas treated, usually by 20% to 25%. These fat cells die and are then naturally processed out of the body.
