Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are used to provide shape and contour in the face. They can be used to restore volume and fullness to regions of the face that are beginning to age, or to accentuate your best features and bring balance and ideal proportions to the face.

Fillers are a great choice for rejuvenating plumpness in the lips. The goal with any of our filler treatments at Kora Aesthetics is to create a perfectly natural, yet transformative result. When fillers are placed properly, they should create desirable contours and a youthful appearance while remaining completely undetectable.

Our expert aesthetic team at Kora Aesthetics is proud to offer Restylane, Juvéderm, Versa, and RHA dermal fillers to help restore volume, create balance, and youthful contours. These fillers are made from hyaluronic acid which is naturally broken down in the skin over time. Fillers generally last anywhere from 6 months up to 2 years and are all fully reversible if an adjustment is needed. Dermal fillers are minimally invasive and begin working right away.
