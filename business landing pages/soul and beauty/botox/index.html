What is BOTOX® Treatment Like?

It’s a simple idea, pausing the repetitive facial movements that cause fine lines and wrinkles. BOTOX® Cosmetic treatment takes precision, medical-grade anatomical knowledge, and skilled expertise with injectables, which you’ll find at soul&beautyMEDx.

BOTOX® temporarily suspends dynamic muscle movement for the skin to repair wrinkles and rejuvenate skin cells. It relaxes the muscles of the forehead, brows, and around the eyes, so when they receive signals to move, they don’t respond.

What Results Can I Expect?

You may notice some diminished dynamic movement in your treatment area right away. It takes BOTOX® an average of 7-10 days to take full effect. After that, your skin repairs damaged cells and smooths out lines and wrinkles. BOTOX® is FDA-approved to last for 3 months before repeated treatment is necessary. For seasoned BOTOX® patients, it may last a bit longer as the muscles acclimate to the medication.
