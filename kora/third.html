Botox / Dysport

Botox and Dysport are wrinkle-relaxing injectable treatments and ideal therapies for getting rid of and preventing fine lines. This 10-15 minute treatment is virtually painless and has zero downtime; results are visible in 3-14 days!

Microtox is another unique aesthetic technique that we use here at Kora Aesthetics for rejuvenating and tightening the face. The Microtox technique targets the skin’s surface, making it more youthful and healthy by reducing pore size, enhancing skin firmness and elasticity, and fading redness. It also aids in the reduction of fine lines, wrinkles, folds, crow’s feet, and other signs of aging.
