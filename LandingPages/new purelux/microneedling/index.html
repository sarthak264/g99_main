Microneedling

Microneedling is a non-invasive skin treatment that stimulates the production of collagen and elastin to enhance skin’s texture and appearance. The method involves rolling a device with fine, precise needles directly on the skin in a controlled motion. In response to superficial injuries, your skin produces more collagen and elastin to heal itself.

Platelet-rich plasma therapy (PRP) is a natural rejuvenation procedure that stimulates healing, collagen production, and the reduction of inflammation. During treatment, blood is drawn from the patient and centrifuged at high speeds to separate the red blood cells, leaving the concentrated plasma. By injecting this platelet-rich plasma into the skin, it will help to improve scarring, reduce wrinkles, and lighten sunspots.
