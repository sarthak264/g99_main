FILLER

As we start to age, the structural components of our skin start to deteriorate, resulting in sagging, loss of suppleness, and premature aging. Dermal fillers are used to eliminate wrinkles, replace soft-tissue volume loss, raise scar depressions, volumize creases and folds of the face, and even plump and hydrate the lips. Dermal filler treatments are designed to produce a natural-looking but noticeable outcome.

Our expert aesthetic team at Pure Lux Med Spa proudly provides dermal fillers such as the Juvederm® Family of Fillers, Radiesse®, and the Restylane® Products and more, to help restore youthful contours, balance, and add more volume to the skin. Your injector will choose the filler based on your needs and aesthetic goals.
