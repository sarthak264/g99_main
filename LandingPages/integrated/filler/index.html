FILLER

Our facial fillers are sterile gels made from a natural occurring sugar found in the skin called hyaluronic acid. They offer a non-surgical alternative to eliminate deeper set-in wrinkles, plump cheeks and lips. This treatment is typically done within a 90 min treatment time. Results can last anywhere from 4-6 months and up to 1 year.

A hyaluronic acid filler is used to fill in deep facial creases, correct severe facial scars, or augment the lips. As we age, skin volume loss and collagen depletion cause our faces to age. The first sign of aging is the loss of volume in the face, which causes hollow cheeks, sunken eyes, and thin lips. As time goes on, skin starts to sag and lose elasticity. Facial fillers are non-surgical alternatives to more invasive procedures like facelifts.
