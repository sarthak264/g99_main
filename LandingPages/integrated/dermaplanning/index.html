Dermaplanning

This treatment is an exfoliation technique involving scraping off the very top layer of skin by scraping it off with a medical-grade scalpel. The shaving process removes dead skin cells and fine vellus hair, referred to as “peach fuzz.”. The process, which stimulates cells to grow, exfoliates the skin, promoting new skin growth.

Patients are left with a fresh, young look, without harsh chemicals, downtime, or pain. Along with removing dead skin cells, this treatment encourages skin regeneration. During the process, the skin tone is evened out and hyperpigmentation, the darker skin spots, diminishes. The skin appears healthier and brighter overall. A patient’s complexion is brightened by smoothing over rough spots on the skin.

A common benefit of this treatment is giving patients a refreshed and rejuvenated appearance. In addition, skin on the face appears softer and younger, and patients seem less stressed and more relaxed. Scalpel facials also provide several other benefits to treating skin imperfections, and some of them are similar to those you’d find in harsher facial treatments.

Dermaplaning is a method for exfoliating the top layers of dead skin cells by slicing them away with a sterile blade at a 45-degree angle. This technique results in a fresher complexion and a smoother skin tone.It involves the removal of the outer layer of dead skin cells and removing fine facial hair called peach fuzz.
