BOTOX® Cosmetic

BOTOX® Cosmetic is a simple, non-surgical procedure that smoothes the deep, persistent lines between your brows that develop over time. One 10-minute treatment – a few tiny injections – relaxes the muscles that cause those lines to form and keeps them relaxed for up to 4 months.

BOTOX® Cosmetic targets one of the underlying causes of frown lines and crow’s feet—the repeated muscle contractions from frowning and squinting over the years. We inject these muscles with BOTOX® Cosmetic to temporarily reduce muscle activity. You will begin to notice a visible smoothing of your crow’s feet lines and frown lines between your brows. BOTOX® Cosmetic has been widely tested. Now it’s approved by the FDA. The results are dramatic and apparent within days

Dysport®

Dysport® is a purified formulation that has been shown to deliver temporary improvements in moderate to severe frown lines, even with repeat treatments. So, the effect of repeat treatments may be comparable to your first use.

With just 1 injection into each of 5 points between and above the eyebrows, Dysport® blocks the signal from the nerve to the muscles, ultimately resulting in a reduction of muscle activity and temporarily preventing contraction of the muscles that cause frown lines.

XEOMIN

Xeomin® is an injectable neuromodulator, or wrinkle relaxer, which temporarily disables nerve impulses to targeted muscles. Similar to Botox®, Xeomin® can help reduce wrinkles caused by frequent muscle contraction. Some common uses for Xeomin® include eyebrow lifting, reduction of frown lines, crow’s feet, nasal squint lines or bunny lines, horizontal lines across the forehead, perpendicular lines between the brows, lines around the neck, and even profuse underarm sweating and migraines.

Kybella®

Kybella® a non-surgical FDA-approved injection treatment to reduce moderate to severe fat on the neck commonly referred to as the “double chin.” Kybella® contains a naturally occurring molecule, called deoxycholic acid, that dissolves fat cells. When injected properly, all other cells are left intact. The body then metabolizes and flushes out the dissolved fat cells. Once these fat cells are eliminated from the body, they are gone for good!

LIP INJECTION AUGMENTATION

If fuller lips are something you’ve always wanted, lip augmentation may be a great option for you. Lip augmentation is a procedure that uses either synthetic products or your own fat to enhance the shape and plumpness of your lips. Upper and lower lips can be treated alone or together at the same time. Very fine blunt-tipped microcannulas are used, and the lips should look and feel natural after treatment. Results are seen immediately.

SCULPTRA AESTHETIC

Sculptra® Aesthetic can help correct shallow to deep facial wrinkles and folds (nasolabial wrinkles and folds) that appear with aging by replacing lost collagen. Not recommended for use in the lips or the periorbital area. Sculptra can also help correct deep wrinkles and creases in the décolletage (chest or cleavage area) that is common among sun-damaged and aging skin.

While not an overnight miracle, it is long-lasting and the first injectable that gives you subtle results over time. Sculptra® Aesthetic works by stimulating the body’s own natural collagen production to replace and multiply lost collagen.

Radiesse®

Radiesse®, formerly Radiance, has a long history of safe and effective use. Use of only a relatively small amount will provide results that will make a lasting difference.No patient pre-testing is required due to Radiesse’s unique natural product formulation and biocompatibility profile. Radiesse will remain soft and pliable and will take on the characteristics of the surrounding tissue, and will offer a durable cosmetic effect to the patient.

Radiesse® can be used to:

  • Repair facial defects caused by trauma or a medical condition by providing structure and support for the skin.
  • Restore bulk to aging tissues.

Restylane® Defyne is a dermal filler injected under the (facial) skin and is for deep wrinkles that need a lift, such as the nasolabial folds (the parenthesis). Once in place, it helps smooth “smile and laugh lines” – the wrinkles and folds (such as nasolabial folds) that may form at the sides of the nose and run down toward the corners of the mouth. Restylane® Defyne preserves the ability to have normal expression and has been shown to maintain effectiveness for the treatment of “smile and laugh lines” for up to 12 months.

Restylane® Lyft (also known as Perlane®) is an FDA-approved cosmetic dermal filler that replaces lost volume and restores youthful contours to the skin to smooth away moderate to severe facial wrinkles and folds1, such as the lines from your nose to the corners of your mouth.
