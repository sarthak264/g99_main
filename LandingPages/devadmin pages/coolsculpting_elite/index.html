WHAT IS COOLSCULPTING

Developed by Harvard scientists, Cool Sculpting® is the only treatment to utilize Cryolipolysis, a revolutionary method of controlled cooling that targets fat cells without compromising surrounding skin or tissues. The patented applicator chills subcutaneous fat cells, causing them to rupture. This triggers an immune response wherein the lymphatic system collects and eventually excretes these destroyed fat cells from the body.

WHAT IS THE FREEZING FAT TREATMENT LIKE?

As the cooling process begins, most patients experience a sensation of intense cold, which soon dissipates into a soothing, painless experience. As the CoolSculpting device works on treated areas, the majority of patients read, check email or even nap during the procedure. Treatments may last anywhere from 35 to 75 minutes, depending on the area being treated. After the procedure, the patient is free to return to their normal, daily activities, including exercise.

IS COOLSCULPTING PERMANENT?

Many patients wonder if after CoolSculpting, can the fat grow back? The simple answer is no. Once the fat is gone, it stays gone. To better understand the science of Cryolipolysis, it is important to distinguish CoolSculpting, a fat reduction treatment, from weight loss treatments. While losing weight causes fat cells to shrink, Cool Sculpting freezes these cells and removes them from the body. The skin and remaining fat adjust to accommodate the reduction of fat volume, resulting in thinner contours and improved definition in treated areas.

Once the fat cells are removed, they are gone, but factors such as excessive weight gain and pregnancy can temporarily obscure your results. Maintaining a healthy body weight after treatment can help you keep your trimmer, more toned figure.

The longevity of CoolSculpting results was proven in a longitudinal study that evaluated patients 9 years after a single cryolipolysis treatment. The researchers found that the patient’s results were still apparent, even after all those years and despite fluctuations in weight. They concluded, “Local reductions in fat have significant longevity…and suggest that results from Cryolipolysis may be very long-lasting.”³

AM I A GOOD CANDIDATE FOR COOLSCULPTING?

Cool Sculpting is not a weight loss solution, nor an alternative for diet and exercise.
