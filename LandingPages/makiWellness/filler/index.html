Facial rejuvenation’s goal is to restore a younger-looking appearance to your face. Facial rejuvenation can be accomplished by using surgical or non-surgical options. Procedures vary in the invasiveness of depth of treatment. Non-surgical processes target certain depths of the facial structures and take care of localized facial concerns such as skin laxity, hyperpigmentation, scars, and wrinkles.

A filler erases wrinkles, plumps, hydrates lips, raises scar depressions, replaces soft-tissue volume loss, and volumizes creases and folds. Age-related skin damage can be accelerated by the sun and muscle movement, which increases wrinkles and fine lines. With dermal facial fillers, we can correct such damage through filler injections.
