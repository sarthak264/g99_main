SmartLipo™ Triplex Laser Liposuction

Liposuction is one of the most sought after cosmetic procedures. SmartLipo™ Triplex and tumescent anesthesia have revolutionized lipolysis in enabling our skilled surgeons at MilfordMD to sculpt your body while awake! Thanks to state of the art technology, SmartLipo™ is the gold standard in laser liposuction treatments.

The SmartLipo™ Triplex is a laser system designed for laser body sculpting and for the disruption of fat cells. It is ideal for treating localized deposits of unwanted fat, fat tissue irregularities and for coagulating tissue leading to a tightening effect. SmartLipo™ Triplex is ideal for treating small areas of the face, neck, arms, male breasts (gynecomastia), and other areas of loose or lax skin (flabby skin). It may be combined with modern liposculpture to treat larger areas such as the abdomen, hips, buttocks, etc. At MilfordMD, Dr. Buckley performs all laser-lipo and liposculpture procedures entirely using local tumescent anesthesia with the patient awake and comfortable.

How Does It Work?

SmartLipo™ Triplex laser-lipolysis is a minimally invasive procedure, which treats fat cells through thermal and photo-disruptive actions. These actions heat and collapse the fat cell membranes resulting in shrinkage of the treated area.

How Many Treatments are Required?

Most patients typically need only one treatment. The session may last from 45 minutes for a small area and longer for larger areas or when multiple areas are treated during one session.

Does Laser-Assisted Lipolysis Produce Lasting Results?

Yes! The SmartLipo™ Triplex procedure destroys the treated fat cells. Adults do not create new fat cells. When a person gains weight, that person’s fat cells simply expand. Should you gain weight, that fat will most likely be deposited in areas where the procedure was not performed.

Am I the Right Candidate?

SmartLipo™ offers contouring and sculpting of the chin, neck, male chest reduction (also known as gynecomastia), female breast reduction, arms, back and side bra bulges, back, abdomen, waist, hips, buttocks, front, back, inner and outer thighs, knees, calves, and ankles.

Since it is a minimally invasive procedure, it does not have the same risks and expense associated with the traditional invasive liposuction surgery. The results are a slimmer, tighter body with minimal downtime, allowing you to get back to normal activities typically in just a couple of days. Ideal candidates are in good health, close to normal body weight and have realistic expectations for the results.
